GUNTUR Kaaram

Guntur Kaaram(pronounced [É¡untur karam]; transl.Spice of Guntur) is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language action masala film written and directed by Trivikram Srinivas, and produced by S. Radha Krishna through Haarika & Hassine Creations. It stars Mahesh Babu, Sreeleela, Meenakshi Chaudhary, Jagapathi Babu, Ramya Krishna, Jayaram, and Prakash Raj.

The film was officially announced in May 2021 under the tentative title SSMB28. Principal photography commenced on 12 September 2022 in Annapurna Studios, Hyderabad. The film's score and soundtrack are composed by Thaman S, with cinematography handled by Manoj Paramahamsa and editing handled by Naveen Nooli.
